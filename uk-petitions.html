UK Parliament Petitions Dashboard

Project description

The UK Government and Parliament Petitions website hosts numerous petitions, each calling for a change in UK Government policy or legislation. Unlike other petition websites, any petition that has over 10,000 signatures receives a written response from the government, while those that have over 100,000 signatures are considered for debate in Parliament.

Data on the number of signatures for each petition is publicly available at the constituency level, but it is difficult to get an overview of petiton data relating to a specific constituency. This information would help MPs better understand and represent the views of their constituents. The purpose of this project is to make this information more easily accessible to MPs and their staff.
