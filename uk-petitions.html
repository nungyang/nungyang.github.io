UK Parliament Petitions Dashboard

  • Go to dashboard

Project description and motivation

The UK Government and Parliament Petitions website hosts numerous petitions, each calling for a change in UK Government policy or legislation. Unlike websites such as change.org, these are only open to British citizens and UK residents. Any petition that has at least 10,000 signatures receives a written response from the government, while those that have at least 100,000 signatures are considered for debate in Parliament.

Each petition webpage has data on the number of people who signed the petition in each constituency. This kind of information is crucial to helping MPs better represent their constituents, but as it stands, it is difficult for MPs and their staff to see key details about their constituency at a glance. The purpose of this project is to make this information more easily accessible to MPs and their staff.
